- `kernel_size`: Size of the 3D convolution window (int or tuple of 3 ints).
- `strides`: Convolution stride (default: (1,1,1)).

- `data_format`: `"channels_last"` (default) or `"channels_first"` for dimension ordering.
- `dilation_rate`: Dilation factor for dilated convolution.
- `groups`: Splits input channels into groups for grouped convolution.
- `activation`: Activation function applied to outputs.
- `use_bias`: Whether to add a bias vector.

5D tensor:  
- `channels_last`: `(batch, spatial_dim1, spatial_dim2, spatial_dim3, channels)`

**Note:** Incompatible to have both `strides > 1` and `dilation_rate > 1`.
