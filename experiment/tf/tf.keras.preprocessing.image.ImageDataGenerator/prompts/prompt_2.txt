### Key Features:
- **Data Augmentation**: Applies random transformations (rotation, shifting, zooming, flipping, etc.) to increase dataset diversity and improve model generalization.

### Main Methods:

3. **`random_transform()` / `apply_transform()`**: Apply random or specific transformations to individual images.
4. **`standardize()`**: Applies normalization (e.g., rescaling) to a batch of images.

- **Use Case**: Previously used with `model.fit()` to feed augmented image data during training, especially when data was limited.
