**Key Functionality:**
- **Input:** Batched boxes (`[batch_size, num_boxes, q, 4]`) and scores (`[batch_size, num_boxes, num_classes]`).
- **Process:** Greedily selects high-scoring boxes while suppressing those with significant IoU overlap, applied per class across all batches.
- **Output:** Returns up to `max_total_size` detections per batch, including:
  - `nmsed_boxes`: Final box coordinates.
  - `nmsed_scores`: Corresponding scores.
  - `nmsed_classes`: Class labels.
  - `valid_detections`: Count of valid detections per batch item (rest are zero-padded).

**Main Parameters:**
- `max_output_size_per_class`: Max boxes per class after NMS.
- `max_total_size`: Overall max boxes retained (across all classes).
- `iou_threshold`: IoU cutoff for overlap suppression.
- `score_threshold`: Minimum score to consider a box.
- `pad_per_class`: Controls padding strategy (per-class vs. total size).

**Note:** Box coordinates can be normalized or absolute; the algorithm is invariant to translation/reflection. Clipping to `[0, 1]` is applied by default.
