- **Parameters:**

  - `depth_multiplier`: Number of output channels generated per input channel.  
  - `data_format`: `"channels_last"` (default, shape: `[batch, steps, channels]`) or `"channels_first"` (`[batch, channels, steps]`).  
  - `dilation_rate`: Dilation rate for dilated convolution.  
  - `activation`, `use_bias`, initializers, regularizers, and constraints are also supported.

**Input/Output Shapes:**
- **Input:** 3D tensor with shape depending on `data_format`.
- **Output:** 3D tensor with channels multiplied by `depth_multiplier`.

**Example:**
```python

```

**Use Case:** Efficiently processes channel-wise spatial features, commonly in lightweight neural networks (e.g., MobileNet).
