- `pool_size`: Size of the pooling window (default: `(2, 2)`).
- `strides`: Step size for sliding the window (defaults to `pool_size`).
- `padding`: `"valid"` (no padding) or `"same"` (pads to keep output size).
- `data_format`: `"channels_last"` (default, shape: `[batch, height, width, channels]`) or `"channels_first"`.

**Functionality:**
- Outputs the maximum value from each window, reducing spatial dimensions.
- Commonly used in CNNs to decrease computational load and extract dominant features.

**Example:**
```python
max_pool = tf.keras.layers.MaxPooling2D(pool_size=(2, 2), strides=(2, 2), padding='valid')
```
