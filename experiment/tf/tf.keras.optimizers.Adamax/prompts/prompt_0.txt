**Key Features:**
- **Algorithm**: Adapts learning rates based on the exponentially weighted infinity norm of past gradients
- **Update Rule**: 
  ```
  t += 1
  m = beta_1 * m + (1 - beta_1) * g
  u = max(beta_2 * u, |g|)
  current_lr = learning_rate / (1 - beta_1 ** t)
  w = w - current_lr * m / (u + epsilon)
  ```
- **Default Parameters**: learning_rate=0.001, beta_1=0.9, beta_2=0.999, epsilon=1e-07

- Gradient clipping (per-weight or global)

- Loss scaling for mixed precision training
- Gradient accumulation for small batch sizes
