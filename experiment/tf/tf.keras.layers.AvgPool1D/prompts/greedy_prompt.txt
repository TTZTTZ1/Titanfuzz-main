**Key Parameters:**
- `pool_size`: Size of the averaging window.
- `strides`: Step size for sliding the window (defaults to `pool_size`).
- `padding`: `"valid"` (no padding) or `"same"` (pads to preserve input length).
- `data_format`: `"channels_last"` (batch, steps, features) or `"channels_first"` (batch, features, steps).

**Input/Output Shape:**
- **Input:** 3D tensor `(batch_size, steps, features)` or `(batch_size, features, steps)` depending on `data_format`.
- **Output:** 3D tensor with `steps` dimension reduced based on `pool_size`, `strides`, and `padding`.

**Functionality:**
- Reduces dimensionality by summarizing local regions, helping to extract dominant features while reducing computation.
- Commonly used in convolutional neural networks for temporal data (e.g., audio, time-series).
