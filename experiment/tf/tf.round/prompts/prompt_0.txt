`tf.round` (or `tf.math.round`) performs element-wise rounding of a tensor to the nearest integer, using **bankers rounding** (round half to even).  

**Key points:**  
- **Input:** A tensor of type `float16`, `float32`, `float64`, `int32`, or `int64`.  
- **Output:** A tensor of the same shape and type as the input.  
- **Rounding rule:** Values exactly halfway between integers are rounded to the nearest even integer (e.g., 2.5 → 2.0, 1.5 → 2.0).  
- **Example:**  
  ```python
  tf.round([0.9, 2.5, -4.5])  # [1.0, 2.0, -4.0]
  ```  
- **Aliases:** Also accessible as `tf.math.round` and `tf.compat.v1.round`.
