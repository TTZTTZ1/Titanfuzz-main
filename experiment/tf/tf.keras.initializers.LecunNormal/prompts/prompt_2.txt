This is designed to maintain stable variance in deep neural networks, particularly with SELU activations.

**Key Features:**
- **Distribution:** Truncated normal centered at 0.
- **Standard Deviation:** `stddev = sqrt(1 / fan_in)`.
- **Purpose:** Preserves variance for stable training, especially with SELU.
- **Usage:** Can be used standalone or as a `kernel_initializer` in Keras layers.
- **Seed Control:** Accepts a seed for deterministic initialization.

**Example:**
```python

layer = tf.keras.layers.Dense(64, kernel_initializer=initializer)
```
