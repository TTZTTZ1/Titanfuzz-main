- **During training**: Normalizes each batch using its own mean and variance, applying learned scaling (`gamma`) and offset (`beta`) parameters.
- **During inference**: Uses moving averages of mean and variance accumulated during training.

**Key parameters**:

- `momentum`: Controls the updating of moving statistics.
- `epsilon`: Small constant to avoid division by zero.
- `center`/`scale`: Enable or disable beta (offset) and gamma (scaling).
- `training`: Boolean argument to switch between training/inference modes.

**Important behavior**: Setting `layer.trainable = False` forces the layer into inference mode, using the stored moving statistics instead of batch statistics.

This layer is commonly used after convolutional or dense layers to reduce internal covariate shift and improve training speed and stability.
