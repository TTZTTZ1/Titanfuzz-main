- `kernel_size`: Size of the depthwise convolution window.
- `strides`, `padding`, `dilation_rate`: Control convolution behavior.
- `depth_multiplier`: Number of depthwise output channels per input channel.
- `activation`, `use_bias`: Optional activation and bias.
- Initializers, regularizers, and constraints for depthwise/pointwise kernels and bias.

**Input/Output Shape:**
- **Input:** 4D tensor `(batch, height, width, channels)` (channels_last) or `(batch, channels, height, width)` (channels_first).
- **Output:** 4D tensor with spatial dimensions adjusted by convolution parameters and channels equal to `filters`.

**Example:**
```python
y = SeparableConv2D(filters=3, kernel_size=4, strides=2, activation='relu')(x)
```
This layer reduces computational cost while maintaining effectiveness, commonly used in lightweight models like MobileNet.
