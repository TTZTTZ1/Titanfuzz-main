\[
\text{MAPE} = 100 \times \text{mean}\left( \left| \frac{y_{\text{true}} - y_{\text{pred}}}{y_{\text{true}}} \right| \right)

- **Usage**: Can be integrated into model training via `compile()` or used standalone.
