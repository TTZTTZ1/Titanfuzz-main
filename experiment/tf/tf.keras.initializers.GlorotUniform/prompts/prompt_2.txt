**Purpose:**  
A weight initializer for neural network layers, also known as the Xavier uniform initializer. It draws initial values from a uniform distribution to help stabilize and accelerate training.

**Key Formula:**  
Samples are drawn uniformly from `[-limit, limit]`, where:  
`limit = sqrt(6 / (fan_in + fan_out))`

- `fan_out`: Number of output units.


