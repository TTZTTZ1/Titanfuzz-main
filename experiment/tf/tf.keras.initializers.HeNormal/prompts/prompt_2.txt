It is commonly used for layers with ReLU activation functions to help mitigate vanishing/exploding gradient problems during training.

**Key Features:**
- **Purpose:** Initializes neural network weights to promote stable and efficient training, especially in deep networks with ReLU activations.
- **Distribution:** Truncated normal distribution (mean = 0, stddev = `sqrt(2 / fan_in)`).
- **Usage:** Can be used standalone to generate initial weight values or specified as the `kernel_initializer` in Keras layers (e.g., `Dense`, `Conv2D`).
- **Seed:** Accepts an optional `seed` for reproducible randomization.
- **Reference:** Based on the work by He et al. (2015) in the paper "Delving Deep into Rectifiers."
