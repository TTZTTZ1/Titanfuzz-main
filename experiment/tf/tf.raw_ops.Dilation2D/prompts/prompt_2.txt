- **input**: 4-D tensor of shape `[batch, height, width, depth]`

- **strides**: List `[1, stride_height, stride_width, 1]` controlling window movement
- **rates**: List `[1, rate_height, rate_width, 1]` for atrous (dilated) dilation

**Operation:**

`output[b, y, x, c] = max_{dy, dx} (input[b, strides[1]*y + rates[1]*dy, strides[2]*x + rates[2]*dx, c] + filter[dy, dx, c])`

**Notes:**
- Each input channel is processed independently with its own filter.
- Max-pooling is a special case when the filter contains all zeros.
- Duality exists: dilation of `input` equals the negation of erosion of `-input` by the reflected filter.
