**output = γ * (x - μ) / √(σ² + ε) + β**, where ε is a small constant to prevent division by zero.

- **Purpose:** Stabilizes and accelerates neural network training by reducing internal covariate shift.
- **Inputs:**

  - `offset` (β) and `scale` (γ): Optional; applied after normalization.  
  - `variance_epsilon` (ε): Small float for numerical stability.
- **Common use:** Often paired with `tf.nn.moments` to compute mean/variance during training, or using moving averages during inference.
- **Reference:** Based on the 2015 paper by Ioffe and Szegedy on batch normalization.
