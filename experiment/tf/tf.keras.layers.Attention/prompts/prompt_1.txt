- **Parameters:**

- **Output:** Attention-weighted output (shape `(batch_size, Tq, dim)`), optionally also returns attention scores.
- **Masking:** Supports query and value masks to exclude certain positions.

This layer is commonly used in sequence-to-sequence models, transformers, and other attention-based architectures.
