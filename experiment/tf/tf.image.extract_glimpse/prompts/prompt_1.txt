- `offsets`: `[batch_size, 2]` tensor specifying the (y, x) center of each glimpse.
- `centered` / `normalized`: Control whether offsets are centered relative to the image and whether they are normalized.
- `noise`: Type of noise for out‑of‑bounds areas (`'uniform'`, `'gaussian'`, or `'zero'`).

**Example:**
