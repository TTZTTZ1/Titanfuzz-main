- **`exclusive=True`**: Excludes the current element, shifting results to the right (first output is 0).
- **`reverse=True`**: Computes the cumulative sum in the opposite direction (from end to start).
- Both `exclusive` and `reverse` can be combined for flexible calculations.

**Example:**
```python

tf.cumsum(x, exclusive=True)     # [0, 2, 6, 12]
tf.cumsum(x, reverse=True)       # [20, 18, 14, 8]
```

- `exclusive`/`reverse`: Boolean flags to modify behavior.
- `name`: Optional operation name.
