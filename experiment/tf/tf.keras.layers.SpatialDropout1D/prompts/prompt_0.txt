- **Input Shape:** 3D tensor `(samples, timesteps, channels)`.
- **Output Shape:** Same as input.
- **Arguments:**

  - `seed`: (Optional) Random seed for reproducibility.
- **Usage:** Apply during training to regularize convolutional or recurrent networks handling sequential data (e.g., audio, time-series).
