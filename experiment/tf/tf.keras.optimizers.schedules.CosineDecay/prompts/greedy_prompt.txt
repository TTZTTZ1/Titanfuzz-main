- **Optional Warmup:** Supports a linear warmup phase (`warmup_steps`) to ramp up the learning rate from `initial_learning_rate` to `warmup_target`.
- **Configurable Minimum:** The `alpha` parameter sets the lowest learning rate as a fraction of the initial rate.
- **Serializable:** Can be saved and restored via Keras serialization utilities.

**Common Use Cases:**
- Training deep learning models with a scheduled learning rate that warms up initially and then decays smoothly.
- Improving convergence in scenarios like fine-tuning or training from scratch.
