- **Inputs:**

  - `segment_ids`: A 1-D integer tensor with sorted values (validated on CPU, not on GPU). Its length must match the first dimension of `data`.

- **Empty Segments:** If a segment has no elements, the output is `0` for that segment.
- **GPU vs CPU:** On GPU, unsorted `segment_ids` may lead to undefined behavior, while on CPU an error is thrown for unsorted indices.

**Example:**
```python

```
Here, rows of `c` are grouped by `segment_ids` (row 0 and 1 → segment 0, row 2 → segment 1), and the mean is computed per segment.
