- `pool_size`: Size of the pooling window (int or tuple of 2 ints).
- `strides`: Step size for sliding the window (defaults to `pool_size`).

- `data_format`: `"channels_last"` (default, shape: `[batch, height, width, channels]`) or `"channels_first"`.

**Input/Output shapes:**
- **Input:** 4D tensor with shape depending on `data_format`.
- **Output:** 4D tensor with downsampled spatial dimensions (height and width reduced unless `padding="same"` and `strides=1`).
