**Key Formula:**  
`loss = mean( (log(y_true + 1) – log(y_pred + 1))² )`

**Purpose:**  
- Penalizes underestimation more heavily than overestimation.  
- Useful when targets have exponential growth (e.g., house prices, population counts).  
- Less sensitive to large outliers compared to Mean Squared Error (MSE).

**Usage Example:**
