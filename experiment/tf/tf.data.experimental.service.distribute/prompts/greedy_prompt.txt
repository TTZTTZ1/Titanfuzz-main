- **Processing Modes:**

- **Coordinated Consumption:** Specify `consumer_index` and `num_consumers` for round-robin reads across synchronized consumers.
- **Integration:** Compatible with Keras (`Model.fit`) and Distribution Strategies.

**Typical Workflow:**
1. Start a dispatcher and workers.
2. Apply `distribute()` to your dataset, specifying the processing mode and service address.
3. Operations before `distribute()` run on tf.data workers; subsequent transformations run locally.

**Use Cases:**  
- Distributed training with balanced data loading.  
- Sharing dataset processing across multiple trainers (using `cross_trainer_cache`).  
- Synchronizing data consumption across replicas in coordinated training scenarios.
