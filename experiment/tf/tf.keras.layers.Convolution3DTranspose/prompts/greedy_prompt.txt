**Key Parameters:**

- `data_format`: `"channels_last"` (default, shape: `[batch, depth, height, width, channels]`) or `"channels_first"` (shape: `[batch, channels, depth, height, width]`).
- `activation`, `use_bias`, `kernel_initializer`, `regularizers`, and `constraints` are also supported.

**Input/Output:**  
Accepts a 5D tensor. Output spatial dimensions are determined by `strides` and `padding`. For example, with `strides=(2,2,2)`, each spatial dimension is doubled.

**Example:**
```python

```

**Use Case:**  
Commonly applied in 3D autoencoders, U-Net architectures, or volumetric generative adversarial networks (GANs) to upsample feature maps.
