- Combines quadratic (for small errors) and linear (for large errors) behavior, controlled by the `delta` parameter.
- Formula: For errors ≤ `delta`, loss = 0.5 × error²; for errors > `delta`, loss = `delta` × |error| − 0.5 × `delta`².
- Default `delta` is 1.0.

**Usage:**

**Common Applications:**
- Reinforcement learning (e.g., Actor-Critic methods)
- Regression problems where outlier robustness is needed

**Parameters:**

- `reduction`: How to aggregate the loss (`"sum"`, `"sum_over_batch_size"`, or `None`).
