**Key Parameters:**

**Important Notes:**
- Automatically applies softmax to `logits`.
- Supports both dense and sparse labels (sparse is faster on CPU/GPU).
- Default blank label index is 0, but can be customized.

**Returns:** A 1-D tensor of negative log probabilities per batch element.
