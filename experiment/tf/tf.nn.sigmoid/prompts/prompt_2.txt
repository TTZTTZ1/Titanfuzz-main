\[
\text{sigmoid}(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}}
\]

- **Input Types:** Supports `float16`, `float32`, `float64`, `complex64`, and `complex128`.
- **Output Range:** For real inputs, the output is always in the interval (0, 1).

- **SciPy Compatibility:** Equivalent to `scipy.special.expit`.


**Common Use Cases:** Logistic regression, binary classification, and as an activation function in neural networks where probabilistic outputs are needed.
