- **Output range:** Maps any real-valued input to the interval \((0, 1)\).  
- **Input types:** Supports `float16`, `float32`, `float64`, `complex64`, and `complex128`.  
- **Behavior:**

- **Common use:** Often used in binary classification and as an activation function in neural networks.
