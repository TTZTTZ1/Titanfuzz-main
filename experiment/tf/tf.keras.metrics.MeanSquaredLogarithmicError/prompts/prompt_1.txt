The `tf.keras.metrics.MeanSquaredLogarithmicError` metric calculates the mean squared logarithmic error (MSLE) between true and predicted values. It is commonly used in regression tasks, especially when predictions span several orders of magnitude, as it penalizes underestimates more than overestimates.

**Key Features:**
- **Formula:** `mean(square(log(y_true + 1) - log(y_pred + 1)))`
- **Usage:** Suitable for regression models where target values are positive and may have a wide range.
- **Integration:** Can be used directly in `model.compile()` as a metric.
- **Sample Weight Support:** Allows weighting of individual samples during calculation.
