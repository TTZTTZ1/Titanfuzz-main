- `data_format`: Channel ordering (`"channels_last"` or `"channels_first"`).

**Input/Output Shapes:**
- **Input:** 4D tensor `(batch, height, width, channels)` for `"channels_last"`.
- **Output:** 4D tensor with upsampled spatial dimensions and specified filters.

**Common Use Cases:**

- Image super-resolution and semantic segmentation.
