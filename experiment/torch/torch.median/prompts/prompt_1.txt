1. **Global median**: Returns a single median value for the entire tensor.

2. **Dimension-wise median**: Returns a namedtuple `(values, indices)` containing medians along a specified dimension.

**Key parameters**:
- `dim`: Dimension to reduce (default: last dimension).
- `keepdim`: Whether to retain the reduced dimension.
- `out`: Optional output tuple for values and indices.

**Note**: For the average of two medians in even-sized cases, use `torch.quantile(input, q=0.5)`.
