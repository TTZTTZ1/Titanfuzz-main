`torch.lu_unpack` reconstructs the permutation matrix **P** and lower/upper triangular matrices **L**, **U** from the packed LU decomposition output by `torch.linalg.lu_factor`.  

**Key parameters:**  
- `LU_data`: Packed LU factorization data from `lu_factor`.  
- `LU_pivots`: Pivot indices from `lu_factor`.  
- `unpack_data` / `unpack_pivots`: Optional flags to control whether **L**, **U** and **P** are returned (default both `True`).  

**Returns:** A namedtuple `(P, L, U)` such that the original matrix can be reconstructed as `P @ L @ U`.  

**Note:** For gradient computation, `torch.linalg.lu` is more efficient than using `lu_factor` followed by `lu_unpack`.
