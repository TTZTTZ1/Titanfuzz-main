## **Key Features**

### **1. Gradient Estimation Methods**

- **Pathwise Derivative (Reparameterization)**: Uses `rsample()` for differentiable sampling via reparameterization trick

- **Exponential Family**: `ExponentialFamily` - specialized for exponential family distributions

- Registry system (`biject_to`, `transform_to`) links constraints to transforms
