**Optimizer State**: Maintains first and second moment estimates with Nesterov correction, supporting momentum decay scheduling via `0.96^(t*ψ)` term.

The optimizer is suitable for training deep neural networks where Adam performs well but may benefit from Nesterov momentum acceleration.
