**Parameters:**
- `input`: Input tensor.
- `p`: Norm power (e.g., 1 for L1 norm, 2 for L2 norm).
- `dim`: Dimension along which to normalize slices.
- `maxnorm`: Maximum allowed norm for each slice.

**Example:**  
For a 3×3 matrix normalized along dim=0 with p=1 and maxnorm=5, rows exceeding the norm are scaled down proportionally, while rows within the limit stay the same.
