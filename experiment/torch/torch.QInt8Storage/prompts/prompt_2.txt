- **`torch.TypedStorage`** and type-specific classes (e.g., `torch.FloatStorage`) are **deprecated**.

- **Use `torch.UntypedStorage` for all new code.**

### **Key Takeaways**
- **Storage is a low-level implementation detail.** Most users should operate on tensors directly.
- **Primary use cases:** Advanced memory management (shared memory, memory-mapped files) or debugging.
- **Avoid direct storage manipulation** in favor of standard tensor operations (`clone()`, `fill_()`, etc.).
- **`UntypedStorage` is the future-proof API;** typed storages will be removed.
