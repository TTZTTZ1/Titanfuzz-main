- **Output:** A real-valued tensor containing the condition number(s), preserving batch dimensions.

- **Note:** On CUDA devices, certain norms (`'fro'`, `'nuc'`, `inf`, `-inf`, `1`, `-1`) synchronize the device with the CPU.
