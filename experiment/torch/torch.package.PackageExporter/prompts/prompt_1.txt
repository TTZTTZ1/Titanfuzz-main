### **Use Cases**
- Deploy models without worrying about dependency conflicts.
- Share reproducible research code.
- Patch or modify code in existing packages.
- Bundle custom resources (configs, binaries) with models.

### **Important Notes**
- **Security**: Packages use `pickle`; only load trusted archives.
- **Isolation**: Packaged modules are name-mangled (e.g., `<torch_package_0>.foo`) to avoid clashes.
