- **Parameterizable Distributions**: Supports various probability distributions (Bernoulli, Normal, Gamma, etc.) with parameterizable properties.

## **Main Components**

### **1. Distribution Base Class**
- Abstract base class with common methods: `sample()`, `rsample()`, `log_prob()`, `entropy()`, `cdf()`, `icdf()`

3. **Probabilistic Modeling**: Building complex distributions via transformations
4. **Bayesian Methods**: Prior/posterior distributions with gradient-based optimization
