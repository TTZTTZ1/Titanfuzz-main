- **Early Stopping**: Non‑reentrant checkpointing (`use_reentrant=False`) can stop recomputation early once needed tensors are available (controlled by `early_stop`).
- **Warnings**:  
  - Always pass `use_reentrant` explicitly.  
  - Ensure the forward pass is deterministic; otherwise gradients may be incorrect.  
  - The reentrant variant does not record the autograd graph during the forward pass and has restrictions on detached tensors and nested structures.

### Use Case:
Ideal for training large models (e.g., transformers, big CNNs) where memory is a bottleneck, allowing you to fit larger batches or deeper networks at the cost of extra compute during backward passes.
