- **Input:** `(N, C, D_in, H_in, W_in)` or `(C, D_in, H_in, W_in)`  
- **Output:** `(N, C, D_out, H_out, W_out)` or `(C, D_out, H_out, W_out)`  
  where:  
  - `D_out = D_in + front + back`

  - `W_out = W_in + left + right`

**Example:**
