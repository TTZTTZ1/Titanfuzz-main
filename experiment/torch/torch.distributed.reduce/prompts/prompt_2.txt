2. Use collective or point‑to‑point communication.
3. Wrap models with `DistributedDataParallel` for data‑parallel training.

## Best Practices
- Use NCCL for CUDA GPU training, Gloo for CPU.
- Ensure each GPU is exclusive to one process when using NCCL.
