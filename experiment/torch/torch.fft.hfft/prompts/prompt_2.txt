- **Input:** A half-Hermitian signal (typically the positive frequencies from a complex FFT).
- **Output:** A real-valued tensor.
- **Parameter `n`:** Specifies the output length. If omitted, defaults to `2*(input.size(dim)-1)` (even-length output). Providing `n` is recommended to ensure correct odd/even signal handling.
- **Usage:** Commonly used to reconstruct a real signal from its half-Hermitian frequency representation.
