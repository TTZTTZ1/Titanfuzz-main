**Key parameters:**
- `input`: Input tensor.
- `q`: Quantile value(s) in [0, 1] (scalar or 1D tensor).
- `dim`: Dimension to reduce (default reduces all dimensions).
- `keepdim`: Whether to retain the reduced dimension.
- `interpolation`: Method for quantile estimation (`linear`, `lower`, `higher`, `midpoint`, `nearest`).

**Example:**
```python
>>> t = torch.tensor([float('nan'), 1, 2])
>>> t.nanquantile(0.5)

```
