### Behavior:
- At the start of each cycle (`T_cur = 0`), LR = `η_max`.
- At the end of each cycle (`T_cur = T_i`), LR = `η_min`.
- After `T_i` epochs, a restart occurs: `T_cur` resets to 0, and `T_i` becomes `T_i * T_mult` for the next cycle.

This approach helps models escape local minima and can improve convergence by periodically "reheating" the learning rate.
