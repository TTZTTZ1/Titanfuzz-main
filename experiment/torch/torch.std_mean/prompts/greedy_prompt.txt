`torch.std_mean` computes both the standard deviation and mean of a tensor along specified dimensions.  

**Key parameters:**  
- `input`: Input tensor.  
- `dim`: Dimension(s) to reduce; if `None`, reduces over all dimensions.  
- `correction`: Degrees-of-freedom adjustment (default 1, Bessel’s correction).  
- `keepdim`: If `True`, keeps reduced dimensions as size 1.  

**Returns:** A tuple `(std, mean)`.  

**Example:**  
```python
std, mean = torch.std_mean(tensor, dim=0, keepdim=True)
```
