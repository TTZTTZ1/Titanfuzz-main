**Key parameters:**
- `a`, `b`: input tensors to contract.
- `dims`: can be an integer `d` (contracts last `d` dims of `a` with first `d` dims of `b`) or a tuple of two lists specifying exact dimensions to contract in `a` and `b`.

**Functionality:**  
It sums over the product of elements along the contracted dimensions, similar to a higher‑dimensional dot product. The contracted dimensions must match in size (broadcasting is supported for non‑contracted dimensions).

**Example use:**
```python
torch.tensordot(a, b, dims=([1, 0], [0, 1]))
```
