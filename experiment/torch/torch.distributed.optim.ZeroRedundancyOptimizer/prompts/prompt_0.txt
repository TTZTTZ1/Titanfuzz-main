   - Can be overlapped with `DistributedDataParallel` for further performance gains.

**Key Notes:**
- Distributed optimizers are **not supported with CUDA tensors** in the current version.
- They help scale training by distributing optimizer computations and memory overhead across nodes.
- Use cases include large-model training, multi-node setups, and memory-constrained environments.
