- **Vh** (conjugate transpose of right singular vectors)

**Notes:**  
- Always returns a tuple `(U, S, Vh)`; use `torch.linalg.svdvals()` for singular values only.  
- Supports float/double and complex dtypes, and batched inputs.  
- Gradients can be unstable for repeated or near-zero singular values.  
- Outputs U and Vh are not unique due to sign/phase ambiguity.
