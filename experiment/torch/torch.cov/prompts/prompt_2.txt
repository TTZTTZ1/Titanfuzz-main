**Key parameters:**
- `correction`: Adjusts degrees of freedom (default 1 for unbiased estimate).
- `fweights`: Integer frequency weights for observations.

- Handles single variables (scalar/1D) by returning their variance.
- Works with 2D matrices for multiple variables.
- Includes Bessel’s correction by default for unbiased estimation.

**Example:**
