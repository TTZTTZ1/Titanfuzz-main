**Key points:**
- **Input:** A tensor with two or more dimensions.
- **Splitting method:**  
  - If `indices_or_sections` is an integer, the first dimension must be divisible by it.  
  - If it is a list or tuple of integers, splits at the specified indices along the first dimension.
- **Returns:** A list (or tuple) of tensors.

**Example:**
```python

torch.vsplit(t, 2)  # Splits into 2 equal parts vertically
```
