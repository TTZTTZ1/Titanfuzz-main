- **GPU training**: Use NCCL (best performance) or XCCL (XPU)
- **CPU training**: Use GLOO

## **Advanced Features**
- **Copy Engine Collectives**: Offload communication to GPU DMA engines (NCCL 2.28+)

## **Best Practices**
- Call `destroy_process_group()` for cleanup

- Set `torch.cuda.set_device()` before NCCL operations
- Ensure consistent tensor sizes across ranks for collectives

## **Launch Utilities**
- `torch.distributed.launch` (deprecated, use `torchrun`)
