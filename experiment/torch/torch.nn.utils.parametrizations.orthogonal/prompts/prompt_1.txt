**Key features:**
- Enforces orthogonality: \( Q^H Q = I \) for \( m \geq n \) (orthonormal columns) or \( Q Q^H = I \) for \( m < n \) (orthonormal rows).
- Supports real and complex matrices.
- Offers three parametrization methods:

  - `"householder"`: Uses product of Householder reflectors.
- Optional dynamic trivialization (`use_trivialization=True`) improves convergence with extra memory.
- Automatically orthogonalizes initial weights via QR decomposition if needed.

**Usage:**
