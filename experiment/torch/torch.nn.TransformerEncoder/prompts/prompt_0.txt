  - `num_layers`: Number of encoder layers to stack.  
  - `norm` (optional): Layer normalization applied after the last layer.  
  - `enable_nested_tensor` (default `True`): Improves performance with high padding rates by using nested tensors internally.

**Important Notes:**
- All stacked layers are initialized with identical parameters; it is recommended to manually reinitialize them after creating the encoder.

  - `src_key_padding_mask` (optional): Mask for padding per batch.  
  - `is_causal` (optional): Hint for applying a causal mask.
- **Output:** Processed tensor after passing through all encoder layers.
