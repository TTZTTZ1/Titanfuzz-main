- `kernel_size`: Size of the pooling window.
- `stride`: Step size for sliding the window (defaults to `kernel_size`).
- `padding`: Adds zero-padding on both sides of the input.
- `ceil_mode`: Uses ceiling (instead of floor) to calculate output length.
- `count_include_pad`: Includes padding in average calculations when `True`.

**Output size formula:**
\[
