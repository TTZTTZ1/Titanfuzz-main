**`torch.copysign(input, other, out=None)`**  
Creates a tensor with the **magnitude** of `input` and the **sign** of `other`, applied elementwise.

**Key behavior:**
- Output magnitude = |`input`|  
- Output sign matches `other`:
  - If `other` ≥ 0.0 → positive output
  - If `other` ≤ −0.0 → negative output
- Supports **broadcasting** and integer/float inputs.
- **Handles signed zeros**: `other = -0.0` results in a negative output.

**Parameters:**
- `input` (Tensor): Magnitude values.
- `other` (Tensor or Number): Source of sign values.
- `out` (Tensor, optional): Output tensor.

**Example:**
```python
>>> torch.copysign(torch.tensor([-2.5, 3.0]), 1)
tensor([2.5000, 3.0000])
>>> torch.copysign(torch.tensor([1.0]), torch.tensor([-0.0]))

```
