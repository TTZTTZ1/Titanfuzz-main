The PyTorch Distributed Elastic Rendezvous system is a distributed synchronization and peer discovery mechanism used to coordinate nodes in a training job. It ensures that all participating nodes agree on the same list of participants and their roles, and collectively decide when training can begin or resume.

1. **Barrier**: Nodes block until a minimum number of participants join, with an optional waiting period to avoid premature completion. If the maximum number is reached, the rendezvous completes immediately.
