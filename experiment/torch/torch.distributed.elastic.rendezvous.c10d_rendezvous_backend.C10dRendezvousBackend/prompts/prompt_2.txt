### Key Functionalities:
1. **Barrier**: Nodes block until at least `min` nodes join, with a small wait time to avoid excluding latecomers. If `max` nodes join, the rendezvous completes immediately.
2. **Exclusivity**: Prevents multiple parallel groups for the same job; late nodes wait until the current rendezvous is destroyed.
3. **Consistency**: All members agree on job membership and assigned ranks (0 to world size), though ranks are not stable across re-rendezvous.
4. **Fault Tolerance**: Handles node failures during rendezvous by automatically triggering a re-rendezvous with remaining healthy nodes.
