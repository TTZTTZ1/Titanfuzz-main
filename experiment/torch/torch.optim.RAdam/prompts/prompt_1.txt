- **Weight Decay Options**:

- **Parameters**: 
  - `lr` (default: 1e-3), `betas` (default: (0.9, 0.999)), `eps` (default: 1e-8)

**Core Behavior:**
- When ρₜ ≤ 5: Performs simple momentum update (like SGD with momentum)
- When ρₜ > 5: Applies adaptive learning rate scaling with rectification factor rₜ
- Automatically transitions from warm-up phase to adaptive updates as training progresses

**Typical Usage:**
```python
optimizer = torch.optim.RAdam(model.parameters(), lr=0.001, weight_decay=0.01)
```

**Additional Functionality:**
- Standard optimizer methods: `step()`, `zero_grad()`, `state_dict()`, `load_state_dict()`
- Hook registration for state dict and step operations
- Parameter group management via `add_param_group()`

The optimizer is particularly useful for stabilizing early training while maintaining the benefits of adaptive learning rates in later stages.
